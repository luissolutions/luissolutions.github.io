Hosting Policy

At Bitveau, we provide hands-on hosting services with a flexible payment model based on your usage, giving you complete control over your expenses. Our hosting contracts are renewable on a 12-month basis, allowing you to adapt to your evolving needs. The cost of the contracts is calculated at a base rate of $0.085 per kilowatt-hour (kWh). Acceptable forms of payment are as follows: Bitcoin, Dogecoin, Litecoin, Ethereum, Bank-To-Bank transfers, Checks, and Wire transfers. Alternative cryptocurrencies will be considered upon request.
