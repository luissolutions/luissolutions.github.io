Consumer + Tech Hybrid Strategy

Eco Lou's core philosophy: combine the defensive stability of Consumer Staples with the growth of Tech Dividend payers, and the income acceleration of High Dividend holdings. Three engines, one coherent system.

  • Consumer Staples — Stability, predictable cash flow, recession-resistant demand, long dividend histories
  • Tech Dividend Growers — High free cash flow, recurring revenue, long-term compounding, dividend growth
  • High Dividend — Current income to reinvest and accelerate compounding
