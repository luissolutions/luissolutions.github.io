Investing Guide: Technology Stocks via M1 App

Introduction to Investing with Apps

Apps like M1 Finance offer user-friendly platforms for building investment portfolios, including Roth IRAs and traditional brokerage accounts. They allow for automation, reinvestment, and fractional share investing.

Dividend & Passive Income Strategies

Dividend Investment Portfolio

Getting Started with M1

M1 lets you build a “pie” of stocks or ETFs with custom percentages. Once funded, M1 automatically invests and reinvests based on your selections. Bank accounts can be linked for recurring or manual deposits.

Roth IRA Strategy Using M1

Roth IRAs grow tax-free. With annual contributions of $7,000 (or more if over 50), investors can build substantial retirement wealth. Dividends reinvested don’t count toward the contribution cap.

Monthly Contributions Example

  • Year 1: $6,300
  • Year 3: $19,860
  • Year 5: $34,811

With compounding growth, a $500/month contribution could reach $735,000 in 30 years.

Portfolio Management & Rebalancing

M1’s dashboard enables you to rebalance, monitor gains, and adjust allocations as your strategy evolves. Dividends are auto-reinvested, and rebalancing helps you stay aligned with your goals.

Investment Tools & Calculators

Use the investment calculator to project future value based on your monthly contributions, interest rate, and timeline. This helps visualize long-term retirement goals.
