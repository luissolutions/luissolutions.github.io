Inventory Management

This SOP outlines the procedures for managing inventory at The Emporium. The goal is to ensure accurate tracking, efficient replenishment, and minimal stockouts.

1. Inventory Tracking

All products must be logged into the inventory management system immediately upon receipt. The following details should be recorded:

  • Category
  • Sub-Category
  • Product Information
  • Quantity
  • Cost per Unit

2. Stock Replenishment

Stock levels should be monitored weekly. If stock levels drop below the minimum threshold, an automatic reorder should be triggered.

  • Minimum Threshold: 20 units
  • Reorder Quantity: Based on average monthly sales
  • Supplier Lead Time: 7 days

3. Product Returns

Returned products must be inspected upon receipt. If they are in resalable condition, they should be logged back into inventory. Damaged goods should be recorded and disposed of according to the disposal policy.

4. Regular Audits

Monthly inventory audits are required to ensure the accuracy of the inventory records. Discrepancies should be investigated and resolved promptly.

Order Processing SOP

This SOP covers the procedures for processing customer orders efficiently and accurately.

1. Order Receipt

Orders are received through the website, Amazon, or eBay. Each order should be reviewed for accuracy, including customer details, product selection, and payment confirmation.

2. Order Fulfillment

Once an order is confirmed, it should be packed within 24 hours. The following steps must be followed:

  • Print packing slip and shipping label
  • Pick products from inventory
  • Pack items securely using appropriate materials
  • Attach shipping label and ensure correct address

3. Shipping

All orders should be shipped using the preferred courier service. Tracking information must be provided to the customer within 24 hours of shipping.

4. Post-Shipping Follow-Up

Customer satisfaction follow-up emails should be sent within 7 days of delivery to ensure a positive experience and encourage repeat business.

Customer Service SOP

Our customer service SOP ensures consistent, high-quality interactions with customers.

1. Response Time

All customer inquiries should be acknowledged within 24 hours. Resolution of issues should be prioritized based on urgency.

2. Handling Complaints

Complaints should be addressed with empathy and professionalism. The goal is to resolve the issue to the customer's satisfaction within 48 hours.

3. Returns and Refunds

Returns should be processed according to the return policy. Refunds should be issued within 7 days of receiving the returned item.

4. Continuous Improvement

Customer feedback should be collected and reviewed monthly to identify areas for improvement in service delivery.
