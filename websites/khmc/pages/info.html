Getting Started with Mods

Step-by-Step Mod Setup:


  1. Download CurseForge: Start by downloading and installing CurseForge from its official website.
  2. Navigate to our server's mod page and download the mod pack specifically curated for our server.
  3. Open CurseForge, go to the "Minecraft" section, and use the "Import" feature to add the downloaded mod pack.
  4. If there are any additional patches, download them and manually place them into the "mods" folder created by CurseForge for the specific game instance.

Following these steps will ensure you have all necessary mods and patches installed for an optimized gameplay experience on our server.
