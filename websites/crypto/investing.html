Capital Gains Tax

Capital gains tax is a tax on the profit made from selling an investment asset such as stocks, bonds, or real estate.

Short-Term vs. Long-Term

  • Short-Term: Assets held for less than 1 year are taxed as ordinary income.
  • Long-Term: Assets held for 1 year or more benefit from reduced tax rates (0%, 15%, or 20%) depending on income level.
