Frequently Asked Questions

Can I visit the facility?

Yes, you can. Please call ahead and bring an ID with you so we can verify your identity. We have a system we must sign you into and log your visit for insurance and security purposes.

Is there insurance for my servers?

For those that would like insurance, we have a policy covering theft, fire, and other acts of God. Clients must be added to the policy as an insured entity.

Do you offer remote hands?

Yes, we do! Reboots and monthly dust maintenance are free, but anything else is charged at a rate of $50/hour.
