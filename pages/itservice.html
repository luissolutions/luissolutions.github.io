IT Services

As an IT Specialist and System Administrator, I offer a wide range of services to manage and maintain your computer systems, ensuring their functionality, security, and optimal performance. Here are some key areas of expertise:

IT Support Specialist

I provide technical support to address hardware and software issues that users may encounter. From troubleshooting problems with QuickBooks, OneDrive, or Windows 10 Pro to ensuring system stability, I am here to assist you. I handle system maintenance, software installations, and network security to ensure smooth operations.

Systems Administrator

I specialize in regular system maintenance and troubleshooting. Setting up and maintaining servers, such as QuickBooks, is one of my core competencies. I also ensure that backup systems are properly configured and secure, minimizing the risk of data loss.

Network Administrator

I manage your company's networks, both local and cloud-based. My responsibilities include ensuring network connectivity, setting up and maintaining networks, and promptly addressing any network-related issues. I prioritize network security and work to establish robust and reliable connections.

Database Administrator

I handle data management tasks, including database backup and recovery. With expertise in database systems, I ensure the integrity and security of your data, implementing efficient backup strategies and maintaining optimal performance.

IT Consultant

I offer consultancy services to assess your company's IT needs and provide suitable solutions. This includes hardware replacement decisions, software upgrades, and setting up reliable backup systems. I help you make informed choices to enhance your IT infrastructure and align it with your business goals.

Cloud Administrator or Cloud Solutions Architect

I specialize in managing and architecting cloud solutions. As a cloud expert, I configure and secure your company's cloud storage and applications. I ensure proper setup, robust security measures, and regular data backups to maximize the benefits of cloud computing.

Website Development, Hosting, and Management

I provide comprehensive website development services, including design, development, and maintenance. Whether you need a new website or updates to an existing one, I can help. I also offer website hosting and management services, ensuring your website is secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally.
